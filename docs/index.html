üèõÔ∏è Government Guidelines & Legal Framework

Juvenile Justice Act, 2015

Kind Hearts strictly follows the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. All children are treated with care and dignity with full safeguarding measures. We ensure proper legal guardianship and foster care arrangements only through registered institutions certified by the government.

Maintenance & Welfare of Parents Act, 2007

We comply fully with the Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Act, 2007. Our elderly care initiatives ensure that senior citizens receive proper maintenance, medical care, dignified living conditions, and regular check-ups as mandated by law.

Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act

Child safety is our highest priority. All registered institutions with us are thoroughly screened and verified. We maintain strict background checks for all caregivers and staff members to ensure zero-tolerance against any form of abuse or exploitation.

Income Tax Deduction (Section 80G)

All donations made to Kind Hearts are eligible for income tax deduction under Section 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act. Donors receive proper tax receipts for audit and financial planning. Donations are fully tax-exempt up to applicable limits.

Charity & NGO Compliance

We operate as a registered charitable organization with complete transparency in fund management and accounts. All partner institutions are verified NGOs with proper registration, government recognition, and valid certifications from relevant authorities.

Data Privacy Protection

Kind Hearts adheres to strict data protection principles and privacy standards. All personal information is encrypted, securely stored in protected servers, and used only for compassionate placement purposes. Privacy of beneficiaries is always maintained.
